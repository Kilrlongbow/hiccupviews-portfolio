Our Story

Seeing what others miss. Capturing what others forget.

"Photography is not about the camera. It's about the eye, the instinct, and the willingness to be present."

Hiccupviews was born from a simple obsession: the moments between moments. The laugh before the performance. The stillness after the crowd has gone. The expression nobody else noticed.

We are a creative media brand rooted in Amsterdam, specializing in event photography, portraits, brand campaigns, festivals, and corporate media coverage. Our work spans the full spectrum of human gathering — from intimate portrait sessions to multi-day festival productions.

What sets us apart isn't just technical skill. It's perspective. We approach every assignment with curiosity and a filmmaker's eye — looking for the story within the story, the detail that reveals the whole.

Clients come to us when they want photography that goes beyond documentation. When they want images that make people feel the room, even from the other side of the world.

Authenticity

Behind the Lens

The Photographer

Founder & Lead Photographer

Based in Amsterdam, the creative force behind Hiccupviews has spent years embedded in the city's cultural fabric — documenting its festivals, its nightlife, its brands, and its people.

With a background spanning photojournalism and commercial photography, the approach combines the spontaneity of documentary work with the precision of editorial craft.

When not on assignment, you'll find them scouting locations, studying light, or somewhere in the crowd — camera in hand, story unfolding.
